Privacy Policy – DoodlePop

Last updated: July 7, 2025

No Personal Data Collected

DoodlePop does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app does not require registration, logins, or user accounts of any kind.

No Internet Connectivity

The app works entirely offline. It does not connect to the internet, track user behavior, or access any online services.

Photos & Artwork

If you choose to save your drawings to your Photos library, DoodlePop will request access to your device’s Photos. These images are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded or shared.

Child Safety & COPPA

DoodlePop is designed for users of all ages, including children. We fully comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Since we do not collect any data, no personal information from children is gathered, used, or shared.

GDPR Compliance

We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). As no personal data is collected, no user consent or data processing is required.
